Hello There

I am currently studying Digital Design at Yrgo Vocational University, focusing primarily on UX and UI design while enjoying illustration. I reside in Kungälv, drawn to its serene environment and older streets, which inspire my affection for vintage art like woodcuts and copper engravings.

My journey has been diverse; from childhood drawings to a brief stint in tattooing as a teenager, then pivoting to welding. This experience taught me prioritization and the importance of clear communication.

I'm immensely motivated by a long-standing passion for design and a desire to move away from dirty workshops. Inspiration surrounds me; I'm particularly inspired by my classmates' rapid growth. I excel in ideation, possess adept drawing skills, and am a quick learner. My fascination with various art forms extends to exploring unique clothing brands, envisioning intriguing website designs. Despite occasional reticence, I am inherently curious about meeting new people.

My ambition post-graduation is to find an engaging design avenue. Eventually, I aspire to venture into entrepreneurship with my partner or join a design agency, driven by the possibilities that lie ahead.
